Travel to Taj Mahal

What is the Taj Mahal?

The Taj mahal is one of the seven wonders of the world. It is located in Agra, which is close to New Delhi. It was built by Shah Jahan as a tomb for his queen. It took more than 15 years to build it.

How to reach it?

If you live outside India-

  • Take a flight to New Delhi
  • Grab a train from Delhi to Agra

If you live in India-

  • Take a direct flight to Agra

    • Or if you can't take a direct flight,
  • Take a flight to New Delhi
  • Grab a train from Delhi to Agra
